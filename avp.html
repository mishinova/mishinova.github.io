Good Life Engine


HOW TO HELP ASPIRING ENTREPRENEURS BUILD HEALTHY HABITS AND ROUTINES FOR PERSONAL GROWTH?


After my first collaboration with Aalto Ventures Programm IN THE entrepreneurial course "Startup experience" and as a fascilitator, we continued working together on a course called "Good Life Engine".

The course launched in September 2019 after months of conceptual work. The format of a nine-months long course offers enough time for participants to try to implement new routines and reflect on how they support their personal growth. In addition, the course offers inspirational workshops and a variety of exercises and tools as support.
