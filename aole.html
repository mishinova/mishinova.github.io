Aalto Online Learning: Storytelling with data


HOW CAN WE SHOW THE GROWTH AND REACH OF THE PROJECT "AALTO ONLINE LEARNING"?


In the spring of 2019, I joined Aalto Online learning, a unit at Aalto University supporting Online learning projects in a grassroots approach since 2016.

I documented its development, gathered additional data such as a rough number of involved people, improved courses, as well as the reach among the students at different schools.

To show its impact, I prepared powerful data visualizations with d3.js with the help of ObservableHQ. Throughout my work, I enhanced my skills in programming and data analysis.
