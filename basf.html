BASF: personalized health plans


HOW CAN WE SUPPORT PERSONILIZED HEALTH PLANS WITH DIGITAL TOOLS?


In addition to my hackathon experience in Finland on the one side as part of the organization Junction and on the other side as a participant in Dash, I also wanted to gain insight into the hackathon scene in Germany. So in February 2019, I participated in the first HackDays Rhein-Neckar. The challenge giver was one of the biggest chemistry companies in the world, BASF.

The idea of our team was to reach towards household heads responsible for the nutrition of the whole family with an IoT device which helps them respect the nutritional preferences and needs of everyone in the family. We came to this solution after numerous rounds of user interviews.
