About me

Hello, my name is Maggie Mishinova and I am based in Berlin and Helsinki. Welcome to my portfolio!


My background is in Psychology and Human-Computer Interaction/Human Factors & Ergonomics and I have experience in Usability Consulting, User Research as well as Data analysis/visualization.

In addition, I have profound knowledge of and passion for the healthcare industry. In the future, I want to work on projects tackling the human error problem.
