IPRally: Establishing UX design process


PATENT SEARCH IS HARD. HOW TO WIN THE TRUST OF IP PROFESSIONALS IN A NOVEL WAY OF SEARCHING?


IPRally is a Finnish LegalTech SaaS startup.

Founded in 2018 by a patent attorney and two Software engineers, it combines artificial intelligence (natural language processing & deep learning) to make patent search more accurate than the existing Boolean and semantic search tools and more user-friendly. The product is an in-browser app available via licensing B2B model. Customers are Patent offices, R&D intensive corporations, patent attorneys and patent search providers.

I joined in February 2020 as the first UX designer in the company laying the foundations for successful user-centered design through close collaboration with end users and the Commercial as well as the R&D team. My work includes:

  • Continuous user research, feedback aggregation & systematisation, driving innovation in a cross-team role
  • Design of features & interactions, communication with the Product owner and developers, QA testing and internal usability testing, UX writing, laying the foundation for a Design system
  • Mapping user journeys
  • Leveraging product analytics to improve the experience
  • Documentation of the product functionality, content writing (newsletters, blog articles)
