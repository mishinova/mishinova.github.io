Junction 2018


HOW DO FINNISH STUDENTS AND VOLUNTEERS ORGANIZE THE BIGGEST HACKATHON IN EUROPE??


Having heard a lot of the volunteering culture in Finland, I was eager to experience it hands-on during my exchange year at Aalto University. So I joined the core team of Junction as an Account manager for the partners on the mobility track. The biggest company I supported was the German Daimler followed by innovative Finnish companies such as Ensto, Rightware and QOCO Systems.

Through the preparation of this event, I could gain insights in how Finns work and organize an event of this size (1200 participants, 50 companies). In my position, I could directly compare the work styles of Germans and Finns as well those of big companies versus smaller ones.
