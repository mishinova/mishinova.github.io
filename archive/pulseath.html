My Startup experience


HOW TO TURN THE THE IDEA OF A SMART T-SHIRT MONITORING YOUR BACK POSITION INTO A BUSINESS?


My first collaboration with Aalto Ventures Programm was for the entrepreneurial course "Startup experience". It is a practical course on entrepreneurship offered by Aalto Venture Programme (AVP) that I did in the spring term of 2019.

I applied as a CEO with a concrete idea for smart fashion tech solution for people with scoliosis. As a CEO, I led a team of brilliant people from diverse backgrounds and many hidden talents who liked my idea and were willing to get deeper into the topics of health, well-being and wearables. Our idea changed soon to wearable fashion for back pain targetting office workers and not the usual professional sportists. The end user testing with our prototype was an inspiring and rewarding experience for me.

Through "Startup experience", I had the opportunity to face many real-life challenges: choose co-founders, establish a motivated team, validate our idea and change it, pitch and negotiate with investors. Approaching the end of the course I facilitated a workshop session on designing your life together with AVP’s experienced trainer Lidia Borisova. It was the beginning of a fruitful collaboration on entrepreneurship and well-being.
