Service Design for S-Ryhmä


HOW TO CREATE VALUE FOR THE CUSTOMERS THROUGH AN EASY TO USE HOUSEHOLD MANAGEMENT ?


In August 2018, I participated in EIT Digital's Summer school on "Retail, Trends, Consumer engagement". The business case we worked on as a team was from the biggest Retailer S-Group (in Finnish: S-Ryhmä) and it was about further consumer engagement through innovative household management services.

The idea of our team was to reach towards people moving in together and combining their incentives at S-group while being offered services to support the moving in like transport. We came to this solution after numerous rounds of user interviews.
